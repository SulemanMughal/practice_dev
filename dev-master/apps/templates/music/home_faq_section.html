Top 3 frequently asked questions

  • 1) What happens when someone in the family forgets to pay?

    Circledin guarantees timely payment each month so that there are no disruptions to your mobile services.

  • 2) What type of data does the Account Owner see?

    Each of our Account Owners are verified and a trusted Circledin community member.The Account Owner will only see high level usuage data such as phone numbers, but not actual text messages, images, etc.

  • 3) Is the premium data per account or per line?

    The premium data is on a per line basis. In other words, you are not sharing data usuages across other members in the family.
