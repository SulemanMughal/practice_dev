Not sure how to get your IMEI? Follow one of the steps below

  1. Enter *#06# on your phone's dial pad.
  2. Check in the settings:
    a. Android: Go to Settings > About device > Status
    b. iPhone: Go to Settings > General > About
    c. Windows Phone: Go to Settings > About > More info
