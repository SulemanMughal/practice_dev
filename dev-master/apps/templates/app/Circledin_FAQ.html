What is CircledIn?

CircledIn is a community of users with a common interest in saving on your monthly expenses. We developed this platform to help individuals save on their monthly expenses so that you can put more money back into your pockets! We also donate 1% of our time to volunteer at a charitable cause and 1% of our profits to a charitable foundation. Read more on our About Us page.

How does it work?

We connect individual subscribers to a family plan to maximize savings. We are like the Uber Pool for mobile plans. Circle into a family and start benefiting the savings with each other.

How is the price set?

We maximize family sizes to give you the cheapest rate available that is offered from any of the premium carriers in the United States, guaranteed.

Will I lose my current mobile number?

No, according to the FCC , Wireless LNP is a wireless consumer's ability to change service providers within the same local area and still keep the same phone number.

What happens when an Account Owner cancels their plan?

To provide a trusted seamless experience we verify all of our Account Owners. Additionally, Account Owners agree to go through a transition of service in the event that they decide to leave for a new carrier. *Currently being an Account Owner is by invite only.

What happens when someone leaves the plan? Does the family plan price go up?

If a member in the family leaves, the price will remain the same regardless if there’s 2 members in a family or 5. We guarantee the lowest price at all times.

I already have a family of 4 or more. How can you help us?

We streamline the payment process so that you’re not requesting payment from your family members each month. Additionally, we’ll handle any issues, upgrades, and will always ensure that your plan is up to date with the maximum savings.

Is CircledIn a mobile carrier or MVNO (Mobile Virtual Network Operator)?

No, we simply connect subscribers to a family plan, handle payment, and provide one of the best customer experiences there is.

Can I get a new mobile number?

Absolutely. After you join a family you will be guided through our questionnaire to ensure you have everything you need.

Will my phone be compatible with the carrier?

We highly recommend visiting the mobile carriers website to see if your current phone is compatible with their network. Feel free to reach out to CircledIn support to get assistance.

How are payments handled?

CircledIn makes payment automatic and seamless. When you are ready to join a circle, you’ll input your payment information on our secure website. CircledIn uses a payment system that is a PCI Service Provider Level 1 which is the highest grade of payment processing security. Each month you’ll be billed for your cell phone line automatically.

Do you run credit checks?

Nope

What if someone in the circle doesn’t pay?

CircledIn, guarantees full payment to the Account Owner each month so that your services are not disrupted when someone forgets to pay.

Why is there a $5 subscription fee?

We kept the subscription fee as low as possible to enable more savings back into your pocket. This subscription fee allows us to keep the lights on and provide you support.

What’s the refund policy?

If you’re not satisfied with our service, we will refund your subscription fee for the month. However, you are responsible for any mobile balances associated with your phone number.

Are there any taxes?

Yes, each carrier has taxes and service fees associated with their plan. For T-Mobile Magenta and Magenta Plus plan, the taxes are already included in the price. For Verizon, you should expect about ~$5 for taxes and service fees.

Do you accept eSIM?

Yes, our plans are directly from the carrier themselves. If your phone supports eSIM then they’ll accept it!

First months Bill

All of our plans are Postpaid. Your first month's bill will include the prorated amount for the month before the Bill Due Date as well as the Nexts Month mobile bill.

Is the data shared amongst family members?

Absolutely not. Each line usage is separate from what your entire family uses. Your line, your data.
